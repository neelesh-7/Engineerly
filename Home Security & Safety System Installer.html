Overview

What do Home Security & Safety System Installers Do?

  Home security and safety system installers install, maintain, or repair security systems, alarm sevices, or related equipment, following blueprints of electrical layouts and building plans. They consult with clients to assess risks and to determine security requirements, inspect installation sites, and study work orders, building plans, and installation manuals. This aids in determining the material requirements and installation procedures that will provide clients with cost estimates for equipment installation. Home security and safety installers may also have to keep informed of new products and developments.

  Home security and safety system installers are constantly in enclosed spaces because of the type of equipment that needs installing and fixing. They continually spend time using their hands to handle and control objects, tools, and controls. Home security and safety system installers must have great interpersonal skills, as they are directly communicating and interacting with clients.
